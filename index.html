The Orb Knows All

In this fun twist on a traditional magic 8 ball, an interactive glowing orb responds to your curiosity through movement and energy. As you guide and "stir" its surface, the orb gradually fills with shifting light until it reaches its peak and reveals your answer. It's part game, part experiment—an atmospheric little experience where your actions shape the outcome. To start playing, click anywhere on the screen. To enhance the experience, turn your volume up to hear mystical sounds.
